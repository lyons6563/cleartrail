ClearTrail — Payroll Contribution Reconciliation & Evidence Engine
The Problem You Already Have

If your company sponsors a 401(k) or similar retirement plan, you already run this process:

Every year (and often quarterly), someone on your team has to prove that:

• Employee deferrals were withheld correctly
• Those dollars were actually deposited
• They were deposited on time
• They were deposited in full
• They were posted correctly

To do that, your team pulls:

• Payroll exports
• Bank/trust statements
• Recordkeeper reports

Then manually builds spreadsheets to match amounts and dates, calculate timing, explain variances, and respond to auditor or compliance questions.

This work is:

• Manual
• Repetitive
• High-risk
• Hard to defend
• Expensive in staff time
• Always under deadline pressure

And if something is wrong, it becomes a compliance problem, not an accounting problem.

What ClearTrail Replaces

ClearTrail replaces the Excel-based reconstruction work.

Instead of manually joining files, building formulas, and hunting for breaks, ClearTrail:

• Normalizes payroll and bank exports
• Reconstructs payroll-to-deposit flows
• Matches deposits objectively
• Calculates business-day timing
• Flags late, missing, and partial deposits
• Quantifies principal at risk
• Produces audit-ready schedules and logs

You upload the same files you already pull.

ClearTrail produces the reconciliation and evidence package automatically.
