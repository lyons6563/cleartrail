# Case Summary – Payroll Contribution Reconciliation

Company profile:
Mid-market employer (≈300 employees) using outsourced payroll and 401(k) recordkeeper.

Objective:
Reconstruct employee deferrals from payroll through trust deposits and identify late or missing remittances.

Source systems:
- Payroll export (payroll.csv)
- Trust/bank activity (trust.csv)
- Recordkeeper posting file (recordkeeper.csv)

Findings:
- 4 payroll periods analyzed
- 1 late deposit
- 2 partial/mismatched deposits
- $83.72 total variance identified

Regulatory relevance:
- Late deposits subject to ERISA 29 CFR 2510.3-102
- Partial deposits indicate unremitted plan assets

Outputs generated:
- Remittance schedule
- Timeliness report
- Exception log
- Evidence index

Result:
Manual Excel process (~20+ hours) replaced by automated reconstruction with confidence-weighted matching.
